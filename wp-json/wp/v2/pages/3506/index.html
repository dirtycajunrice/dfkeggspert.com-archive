Lore<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Wolves can be found from the snowy tundra of Crystalvale to the deep woods of the Fairling Forest.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Early in their life, they\u2019re susceptible to mana that surrounds them. The type of mana they\u2019re exposed to changes the traits they develop as they grow into adulthood.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Gaining the respect of a wolf is a dangerous and challenging task, though made easier if befriended as a pup. In these situations, they can become life-long companions.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(Part of the Wolves family, which includes Direwolves)<\/p>\n\n\n\n
